# Email & Outreach Templates — Healthcare Hiring Signal Detection

## Target Personas
- Recruiters
- TA (Talent Acquisition) Heads
- Staffing agency operators
- Ops leaders in healthcare organizations

## Email Sequence Overview

| Step | Day | Goal |
|---|---|---|
| Email 1 | Day 0 | Context + relevance (reference their post) |
| Email 2 | Day 2 | Problem framing (amplify pain) |
| Email 3 | Day 4 | Value + proof (how others solve it) |
| Email 4 | Day 7 | Soft CTA (15-min call) |
| Email 5 | Day 10 | Breakup (create urgency) |

---

## Email 1 — Contextual Hook

**Subject:** Saw your hiring post – quick question

**Body:**
```
Hi {{first_name}},

Came across your post about hiring {{hiring_role}} — looks like you're actively scaling your team.

We've been working with healthcare teams facing similar hiring spikes, especially for roles like {{hiring_role}}, where speed and quality both matter.

Curious — are you currently able to fill these roles fast enough, or is it becoming a bottleneck?

– {{sender_name}}
```

**Variables:** first_name, hiring_role, sender_name

---

## Email 2 — Pain Amplification

**Subject:** Re: hiring {{hiring_role}}

**Body:**
```
Hi {{first_name}},

Following up — one pattern we're seeing across healthcare hiring:

Even when demand is high, teams struggle with:
- Inconsistent candidate quality
- Delays in screening
- Drop-offs before joining

This usually slows down hiring much more than expected.

Are you seeing something similar on your end?

– {{sender_name}}
```

**Variables:** first_name, hiring_role, sender_name

---

## Email 3 — Value Proposition

**Subject:** How teams are fixing this

**Body:**
```
Hi {{first_name}},

Some teams we work with solved this by automating parts of sourcing + screening, while keeping control on final decisions.

This helped them:
- Reduce time-to-hire significantly
- Improve candidate quality
- Handle higher volumes without scaling recruiters

Happy to share what's working if relevant.

– {{sender_name}}
```

**Variables:** first_name, sender_name

---

## Email 4 — Soft CTA

**Subject:** Worth exploring?

**Body:**
```
Hi {{first_name}},

If hiring demand is still high on your side, this might be worth a quick discussion.

Would it make sense to compare notes for 15 mins this week?

– {{sender_name}}
```

**Variables:** first_name, sender_name

## Email 5 — Breakup

**Subject:** Should I close this?

**Body:**
```

Not sure if this is relevant right now.

If hiring is already under control, I'll close this out.

If not, happy to reconnect later when it becomes a priority.

– {{sender_name}}
```

**Variables:** first_name, sender_name

## LinkedIn Outreach Sequence (Optional but Recommended)

| Step | Action | Message Guidance |
|---|---|---|
| Connect | Send connection request | Reference their specific hiring post. Keep under 300 chars. |
| Follow-up | DM after acceptance | Ask about their hiring challenges. Reference the role they're hiring for. |
| CTA | Book a call | Short, specific ask — 15 min call to compare notes. |

### Personalization Rules
- **Always** reference the specific post/role they mentioned
- **Never** open with what you do — open with what they're dealing with
- **Speed matters** — outreach within 24h of post has highest conversion
- **Staffing agencies are multipliers** — prioritize them (1 agency = many roles)

## Conversion Factors

| Factor | Impact |
|---|---|
| Real-time signal (24h posts) | Very high |
| Personalization (post reference) | Critical |
| Targeting staffing agencies | Multiplier |
| Speed of outreach | Decisive |
