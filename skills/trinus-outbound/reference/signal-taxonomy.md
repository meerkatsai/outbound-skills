# Signal Taxonomy — Trinus Outbound

Signals indicate that a company is likely entering a buying window for IT consulting, data analytics, cloud engineering, or staffing services. Not all signals are equal — use the priority tiers below to focus outreach effort.

---

## Signal Priority Tiers

### Tier 1 — Act Within 48 Hours (highest intent)

| Signal | What It Looks Like | Why It Matters for Trinus | Trinus Service Angle |
|---|---|---|---|
| **Acquisition / Merger Announced** | Press release: "[Company A] acquires [Company B]" | Post-merger IT and data integration is inevitable — two systems must become one. Window opens immediately, decisions made in first 90 days. | Data Management, BI/Analytics, IT Consulting |
| **New CDO / CTO / CIO Hired** | LinkedIn announcement, press release: "John Smith joins [Company] as Chief Data Officer" | New leaders evaluate and commit to vendors fast. They want quick wins. The first 60 days are critical. | Any service matching their stated mandate |
| **Digital Transformation Initiative Announced** | PR: "[Company] launches $50M digital transformation program" | Budget is committed, timeline is set, vendor selection is happening now. | Cloud Engineering, IT Consulting, Staffing |
| **Cloud Migration Announced** | "[Company] migrates to AWS/Azure" or job postings for cloud architects | Active migration = active need for cloud engineering expertise and staffing. | Cloud Engineering, Staffing |

### Tier 2 — Act Within This Week

| Signal | What It Looks Like | Why It Matters for Trinus | Trinus Service Angle |
|---|---|---|---|
| **Hiring Spike in Data/IT Roles** | 5+ open roles for Data Engineer, BI Developer, Cloud Architect, Data Scientist | Company is capacity-constrained. They're trying to build internally what Trinus delivers faster and cheaper. | Staffing, Managed Services |
| **New BI / Analytics Platform Deployment** | Press release or blog: "We're deploying Snowflake/Tableau/Power BI" | Platform selection is done; now they need implementation support and ongoing management. | BI/Analytics, Data Management |
| **Compliance Deadline or Audit** | Industry news: new FDA regulation, SOX audit cycle, HIPAA update | Compliance creates hard deadlines that can't slip. Data governance and reporting tooling is urgently needed. | Data Management, IT Consulting |
| **Funding Round Announced** | "[Company] raises $X in Series B/C/D" | Post-raise = scaling pressure. Investors expect GTM and operational scale-up. Usually triggers IT and data infrastructure investment. | Cloud Engineering, IT Consulting |

### Tier 3 — Add to Nurture Queue (low urgency, worth monitoring)

| Signal | What It Looks Like | Why It Matters | Trinus Service Angle |
|---|---|---|---|
| **Leadership Change Below C-Suite** | New VP Analytics, Director of IT, Head of Data Engineering | Influencer-level change may precede budget reallocation. Worth a light touch. | Match to their function |
| **New Office / Geographic Expansion** | "[Company] opens new office in [city]" | New location often means IT build-out and staffing. | Staffing, IT Consulting |
| **Product Line Expansion** | "[Company] launches [new product]" | New products need data pipelines, telemetry, analytics. | BI/Analytics, Cloud Engineering |
| **Strategic Partnership Announced** | "[Company A] partners with [Company B] for data sharing" | Data sharing arrangements require integration work. | Data Management |

## Signal Freshness Rules

- **M&A signals:** Contact within 2 weeks of announcement. Post-merger vendor evaluation typically happens within 30-60 days.
- **Leadership change:** Contact within 30 days of appointment. The 90-day window starts fast.
- **Hiring spike:** Contact while postings are active. Close when roles are filled.
- **Digital transformation:** Contact within 60 days of announcement. Budget commitment window.
- **Signals older than 90 days:** Downgrade priority. Refresh search to find newer developments before reaching out.
